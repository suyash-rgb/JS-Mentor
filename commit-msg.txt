fix: cloudinary folder synchronization

Bridged the gap between the Scheduling Engine and the Cleanup Service:

- Folder Synchronization: The run_scheduling_engine now automatically populates the cloudinary_folder field in the Doubt record at the moment a session is created.
- Standardized Pathing: All session assets are now explicitly mapped to js-mentor/sessions/{sessionId}.
- Guaranteed Deletion: When a trainer clicks "Resolve Session", the backend now has a guaranteed path to the session's folder. The cleanup task will now successfully find and permanently delete all images associated with that specific interaction.
