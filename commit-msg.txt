refactor: service level refactoring

Curriculum Service (app/services/curriculum_service.py):

Moved complex logic for CSV quiz imports, Cloudinary video uploads, and video updates out of the curriculum.py router.
The router now only handles request parsing and passes data to the service.

Trainer Service (app/services/trainer_service.py):

Moved session resolution and availability toggling logic to the service layer.
Integrated reactive scheduling triggers and WebSocket broadcasts directly into the service functions.

Scheduling Service (app/services/scheduling_service.py):

Created a new service to handle doubt registration, queue retrieval, and trainer session management.
The scheduling.py router is now a thin wrapper around these service calls.

Auth Service (app/services/auth_service.py):

Moved Clerk webhook synchronization logic (sync_clerk_user_to_db) into the service layer.
Cleaned up the auth.py router to focus solely on endpoint definition and signature verification.
