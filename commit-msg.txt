feat: Complete Trainer Authentication Refactor

This commit finalizes the separation of the public student authentication (via Clerk) and the internal institute trainer authentication (via custom JWT).

Key Changes:
- **Port Forwarding Support**: Updated `package.json` start/dev scripts to bind to `0.0.0.0`, fixing IDE port forwarding issues.
- **Removed Scholar Number**: Removed the "Scholar Number" field entirely from the Trainer Registration and Login flows as it's no longer applicable.
- **Trainer Auth Routing**: 
  - Created `TrainerProtectedRoute.js` to specifically handle JWT-based route protection.
  - Updated `AppRouter.js` to route `/trainer/dashboard` through the new protector to prevent Clerk from overriding it.
- **Global Trainer Access**: Refactored the core `ProtectedRoute.js` to respect trainer JWT sessions. Trainers can now freely access the AI Compiler, JS Compiler, and all curriculum modules without being redirected to Clerk.
- **Dynamic Navbar State**: The Navbar now detects trainer sessions, replacing the "Login/Sign Up" buttons with a fully functional red "Sign Out" button (and clearing local storage upon exit).
- **Terminology Updates**: Replaced legacy "Enrolled with our institute?" messaging across Clerk and Trainer auth pages with clearer text ("Are you a Trainer?", "Sign Up Here") to prevent user confusion.
