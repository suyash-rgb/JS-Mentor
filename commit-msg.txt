Quiz Data Structure Account:
Quizzes are stored nested inside the 'pageContent' of lesson links within data.json. This approach was chosen to ensure a linear relationship between lesson content and assessments, maintaining consistency with existing 'exercises' data. This avoids bloating the SQL database and keeps curriculum reads efficient.
